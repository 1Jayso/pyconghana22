What Is Pycon Ghana?

Pycon Ghana is national gathering of Python Developers to learn, collaborate, and share experiences within the Python ecosystem.
Python Ghana is the main organizer for Pycon Ghana.

Pycon Ghana seeks to bring together professionals, industy experts, student and enthusiasts, core developers, sysadmins and operations folks to learn about new trends in the python language. During the conference Talks, Tutorial sessions and Sprints are organized to help train and educate both developers and promming enthusiasts who have affinity for the Python Programming language.

PyCon Ghana is supported by the PSF, and the revenue generated by PyCon Ghana is critical to the community's continued support and operations. Because PyCon Ghana is supported by the non-profit PSF, we keep registration costs much lower than comparable technology conferences, allowing PyCon Ghana to be accessible to the broadest possible audience in Ghana.

We are thrilled to be welcoming everyone back to PyCon Ghana in person for the first time in three years. Our community's safety is our top priority, and we will keep you updated as the situation evolves. We will implement Health and Safety Guidelines for all attendees to follow during the event in order to allow our community to reconnect in a safe manner.
